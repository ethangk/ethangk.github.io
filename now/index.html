Heading North

I’m in the process of moving to Sheffield. Over the past year, I’ve found myself craving being closer to the outdoors. I’m going to give it a trail for 6 months before deciding what makes sense for me. It’s an opportunity to do more outdoorsy habits, and attempt to integrate and actively participate in community, something I’ve had difficulty finding in London.
